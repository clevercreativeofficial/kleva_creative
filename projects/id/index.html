Eco Market: Sustainable E-Commerce Platform

A complete redesign of an e-commerce platform specializing in sustainable products, focusing on intuitive navigation and a clean, earthy aesthetic that reflects the brand's values.

Client Eco Market Inc.
Year 2023
Services UI/UX Design, Web Development

Project Overview

Eco Market approached me with the challenge of redesigning their outdated e-commerce platform. Their existing website was difficult to navigate, didn't reflect their sustainable ethos, and had a high cart abandonment rate.

The goal was to create a digital experience that would:

  • Better communicate their brand values of sustainability and ethical sourcing
  • Improve product discoverability and navigation
  • Streamline the checkout process to reduce abandonment
  • Create a responsive design that worked equally well on desktop and mobile

Over a period of 12 weeks, I worked closely with the Eco Market team to research, design, and develop a solution that increased their conversion rate by 35% and reduced bounce rates by 40%.

Research

Understanding the User

User Interviews

I conducted interviews with 15 existing and potential customers to understand their shopping behaviors, pain points, and what they look for in sustainable products.

Key findings included:

  • Users wanted clearer sustainability information at the product level
  • Many were frustrated by "greenwashing" and wanted verifiable claims
  • Mobile users found the current site difficult to navigate
  • Price comparison was important even for eco-conscious shoppers

Competitive Analysis

I analyzed 8 competing sustainable e-commerce platforms to identify industry standards and opportunities for differentiation.

Insights included:

  • Most competitors used earthy color palettes but lacked visual distinction
  • Only 2 sites had dedicated sustainability filters
  • Checkout processes averaged 3-5 steps across competitors
  • Product pages varied widely in how they presented sustainability information
Design

Design Solutions

Information Architecture

Restructured the navigation to prioritize sustainability categories while maintaining traditional product browsing. Added filters for sustainability attributes.

Visual Design

Created an earthy but vibrant color palette that stood out from competitors while maintaining accessibility standards.

Mobile-First Approach

Designed all components mobile-first, with special attention to touch targets and thumb-friendly navigation.
