E-Commerce Redesign

Eco Market: Sustainable Shopping Platform

A complete redesign of an eco-conscious e-commerce platform that increased conversions by 35% while better communicating the brand's sustainability mission.

Client Eco Market Inc.
Year 2023
Services UX Research, UI Design, Front-end
Explore Case Study
Visit live site
Project Duration 12 Weeks

Project Overview

Eco Market, a pioneer in sustainable e-commerce, approached us with a challenge: their existing platform wasn't effectively communicating their environmental values or converting visitors. Despite offering ethically-sourced products, their outdated design and cumbersome checkout process were hindering growth.

The Challenge

  • Confusing navigation that buried sustainable product categories
  • Lack of clear sustainability indicators on product pages
  • 5-step checkout process with 68% abandonment rate

Our Approach

  1. 1 Discovery: User interviews, competitive analysis, and analytics review
  2. 2 Strategy: Information architecture restructuring and user flows
  3. 3 Design: Wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing
User Research

Understanding the Eco-Conscious Shopper

Key User Insights

1

Sustainability Transparency

82% of users wanted clearer product sustainability credentials but distrusted vague claims

2

Mobile-First Behavior

60% of traffic came from mobile, but only 22% of conversions

Competitive Analysis

Visual Differentiation

Most competitors used predictable "earthy" palettes without distinct branding

Filter Limitations

Only 25% offered sustainability-focused filtering options

Design Solutions

Creating a Sustainable Shopping Experience

Restructured Navigation

We completely reorganized the information architecture to prioritize sustainability attributes while maintaining traditional shopping categories.

Key improvements:

  • Added "Shop by Values" as primary navigation item
  • Implemented sustainability badges with verification details
  • Created "Impact Dashboard" showing collective customer savings

Measurable Results

The redesign delivered significant improvements across key performance indicators

40%

Reduction in bounce rate
