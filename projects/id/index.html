E-Commerce Redesign

Eco Market: Sustainable Shopping Experience

A complete redesign of an eco-conscious e-commerce platform that increased conversions by 35% while better communicating the brand's sustainability mission.

Client Eco Market Inc.
Year 2023
Services UX Research, UI Design, Front-end Development
View Key Results

Project Overview

Eco Market, a pioneer in sustainable e-commerce, approached us with a challenge: their existing platform wasn't effectively communicating their environmental values or converting visitors. Despite offering ethically-sourced products, their outdated design and cumbersome checkout process were hindering growth.

The Challenge

The existing website suffered from several critical issues:

  • Confusing navigation that buried sustainable product categories
  • Lack of clear sustainability indicators on product pages
  • 5-step checkout process with 68% abandonment rate
  • Mobile experience that accounted for 60% of traffic but only 20% of conversions

Our Approach

We implemented a human-centered design process over 12 weeks:

  1. Discovery: User interviews, competitive analysis, and analytics review
  2. Strategy: Information architecture restructuring and user flows
  3. Design: Wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing
  4. Development: Responsive front-end implementation

Project Highlights

  • Timeline 12 weeks (Research to Launch)
  • Team UX Designer, UI Designer, Front-end Developer
  • Platform Shopify Plus
  • Key Metrics 35% conversion increase, 40% bounce rate reduction

User Research

Understanding the Eco-Conscious Shopper

Key User Insights

1

Sustainability Transparency

82% of users wanted clearer product sustainability credentials but distrusted vague claims

2

Mobile-First Behavior

60% of traffic came from mobile, but only 22% of conversions

3

Checkout Frustrations

68% abandoned carts, citing "too many steps" and "unexpected costs"

Competitive Analysis

Visual Differentiation

Most competitors used predictable "earthy" palettes without distinct branding

Filter Limitations

Only 25% offered sustainability-focused filtering options

Checkout Efficiency

Average 3.8 steps to complete purchase across competitors

Design Solutions

Creating a Sustainable Shopping Experience

Restructured Navigation

We completely reorganized the information architecture to prioritize sustainability attributes while maintaining traditional shopping categories.

Key improvements:

  • Added "Shop by Values" as primary navigation item
  • Implemented sustainability badges with verification details
  • Created "Impact Dashboard" showing collective customer environmental savings
  • Reduced main menu items from 12 to 7 for clarity

Visual Identity Refresh

While maintaining brand recognition, we evolved the visual language to better communicate sustainability without clichés.

Design system highlights:

  • Expanded color palette with accessible contrast ratios
  • Custom sustainability icons for quick scanning
  • Photography style emphasizing real people and product origins
  • Clear typography hierarchy for better content scanning
